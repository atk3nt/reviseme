## üé® Design Recommendations

### **Color Usage:**
- **Primary Blue (#0066FF)**: CTAs, links, primary buttons
- **Hover Blue (#0052CC)**: Button hover states
- **Text Dark (#001433)**: Headlines, important text
- **Text Medium (#003D99)**: Body text, descriptions
- **Background Light (#E5F0FF)**: Section backgrounds, cards

### **Typography:**
- **Font**: DM Sans
- **H1**: 64px (desktop) / 48px (tablet) / 40px (mobile) - Bold (700)
- **H2**: 48px / 36px / 32px - SemiBold (600)
- **H3**: 32px / 24px / 24px - SemiBold (600)
- **Body**: 16px - Regular (400)
- **CTA Buttons**: 18px - SemiBold (600)

### **Spacing:**
- **Section Padding**: 64px (desktop) / 48px (tablet) / 32px (mobile)
- **Element Gap**: 24px between major elements, 16px between related items
- **Container Padding**: 8px from viewport edges

### **Button Styles:**
- **Primary CTA**: Blue (#0066FF) background, white text, rounded corners, padding 16px 32px
- **Hover State**: Darker blue (#0052CC)
- **Secondary**: White background, blue border, blue text

## üöÄ Conversion Optimization Tips

### **Above the Fold:**
1. **Clear value proposition** in headline
2. **Single, prominent CTA** (Get Started Free)
3. **Trust indicators** (money-back guarantee, no credit card)
4. **Visual** showing the product in action

### **CTA Placement:**
- Hero section (primary)
- After "How It Works" section
- After Features section
- After Pricing section
- Final CTA before footer

### **Trust Building Elements:**
- Money-back guarantee prominently displayed
- Secure payment badges
- Clear pricing (no hidden fees)
- FAQ addressing common concerns
- Subject/exam board coverage (shows expertise)

### **User Journey:**
1. **Landing** ‚Üí Understand value prop
2. **Problem/Solution** ‚Üí Relate to pain points
3. **How It Works** ‚Üí See it's simple
4. **Features** ‚Üí Understand benefits
5. **Subjects** ‚Üí See it covers their needs
6. **Pricing** ‚Üí Clear, affordable, risk-free

8. **Final CTA** ‚Üí Convert
