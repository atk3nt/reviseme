## üéØ Conversion-Optimized Structure (Recommended Order)

### **Psychology & Conversion Reasoning:**

**Principle 1: Hook ‚Üí Value ‚Üí Proof ‚Üí Action**
- Hook (Hero) ‚Üí Show value (Features) ‚Üí Build trust (Proof) ‚Üí Convert (Pricing/CTA)

**Principle 2: Address Objections Early**
- Show features before pricing (so they understand value)
- Address concerns (FAQ) before final CTA

**Principle 3: Multiple Conversion Points**
- Early CTA (hero) for quick converters
- Mid-funnel CTA (after features) for those who need more info
- Final CTA (after FAQ) for those with concerns
